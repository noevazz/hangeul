Certical and horizontal vowels:

A vertical vowel has the vertical stroke longer than the other strokes: ÏïÑ

A horizontal vowel has the horizontal stroke longer than the other strokes: Ïò§

💡 „Öá must be beside the vowel if this is a vertical vowel, and on top of the vowel if this is an horizontal vowel.
