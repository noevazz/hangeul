What is hangeul?, hangeul is the korean alphabet


In korean, words are formed by joining syllables, just as in english: alphabet (al-pha-bet), hangeul (han-geul)

2. In korean, syllables must have at least one vowel AND one consonant:

The syllable 아 (a), it sounds like: the "a" in car (you will learn more about vowels and consonants in detail later)

3. The "ieung" consonant ㅇ:

★This consonant is silent at the beginnig: 아 (ㅏ is the "a" vowel)

★When used at the end, it makes an "ng" sound (sing), you will learn more about this later, don't worry :)
